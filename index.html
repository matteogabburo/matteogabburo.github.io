About

I'm Matteo Gabburo, CTO of Aithlas, where I lead the design and deployment of AI-driven solutions for industrial clients. I hold a PhD in Information and Communication Technology from the University of Trento, where my research focused on loss and reward functions for Generative Question Answering systems under the supervision of Prof. Alessandro Moschitti.

My research spans training generative QA models with weak supervision techniques, developing automatic evaluation systems for QA, and designing efficient pre-training tasks for Transformer models.
